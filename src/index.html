Implicit Association Test

Who Did It

In this task, you will be asked to categorize pictures into groups as fast as you can. In the pictures you will be shown, one person committed a criminal offense in Berkeley, and the other four are innocent. You will rank the pictures of the people you're shown based on their likelihood of being the criminal. At the end of the first task, you can take all the time you need to tell us what you think about each of the people you were shown in the first task. We are interested in your honest opionions.
